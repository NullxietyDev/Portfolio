Game Description

Zentera is a cozy voxel puzzle adventure where you play as Gori the lizard, using his stretchy tongue to solve environmental puzzles and collect trinkets to impress his partner. The game combines charming character-driven storytelling with clever puzzle mechanics in a beautifully crafted voxel world.
